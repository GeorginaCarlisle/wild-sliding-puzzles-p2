Instructions

  1. Click on a tile to slide it into the empty space
  2. Only tiles next to an empty space can be moved
  3. Slide the tiles around so that one by one they slot into their correct position (this will take a number of moves)
  4. When correctly placed the tiles will form an image
  5. Have a go, play around and see if you can find a way to get all the tiles into the correct position

If you are feeling stuck:

  • See what the completed puzzle looks like by clicking the Sneaky Peek button
  • Or get some tips by clicking the Tips button

Tips

  1. First work on getting the top row in place
  2. Determine the 3 tiles that will sit together at the top. Use the sneaky peek if you need to
  3. Get the 3 tiles in the right order. They do not need to be at the top yet
  4. Snake the tiles into position, following one after the other
  5. Now you can work on the rest of the tiles
  6. Once again start by figuring out where the tiles need to go
  7. Then get the tiles in the right order
  8. Snake them round into the correct position
  9. And... Hey Presto! You have a completed puzzle
