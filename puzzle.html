Instructions

  1. Click on a tile to slide it into the empty space
  2. Only tiles next to an empty space can be moved
  3. Slide the tiles around so that one by one they slot into their correct position (this will take a number of moves)
  4. When correctly placed the tiles will form an image
  5. Have a go, play around and see if you can find a way to get all the tiles into the correct position

If you are feeling stuck:

  • See what the completed puzzle looks like by clicking the Sneaky Peek button
  • Or get some tips by clicking the Tips button
